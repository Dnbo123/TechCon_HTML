TechCon's history

TechCon was founded in the year 2007 by Sir Don Bosco, who was 27 years at that time.Since then, the organization has been on a continous growth trajectory and as of now it has a footprint in 40 countries.

Through the years TechCon has evolved to become major player in global technological advancement.While on our road to achieve this, TechCon has attained a number of achievements over the years.You can find some highlights of our years below.

  • 2011 - TechCon achieves its milestone of funding 2000 start-ups
  • 2014 - Of the 2900 startups that were funded by TechCon, more than 2300 succesffly reached a 1 million dollar valuation
  • 2017 - TechCon was listed as the 5th fastest growing company globally by times magazine

TechCon reveives over 20,000 funding request every year. Out of this, only 1000 companies successsfully pass the vetting stage in order to receive funding.
