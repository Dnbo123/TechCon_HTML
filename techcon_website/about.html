History of TechCon

TechCon conference was founded in the year 2010 in the Capital of Nairobi by Sir Don Bosco as a platform for Techies to showcase their innovative ideas.Over the years, TechCon has been able to catapult over 10,000 tech start-ups through funding and bringing in industry leads to facilitate the journey

The faciitating of techCon was facilitated by a number of entities

  • 2010 - Founded by Sir Don Bosco
  • 2013 - Got it's first major funding ($800,000)
  • 2015 - Successfully facilitated thed Founding of 2500+ tech start-ups
  • 2021 - Successfully facilitated the founding of 10,000+ tech start ups
